Marketplace Du'a — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Marketplace Du'a does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. Everything the extension does happens locally in your own browser.

What the extension stores

The extension saves only your own settings so it can work the way you configured it:

  • Whether reminders are turned on or off
  • How often the reminder is shown
  • Your dark-mode preference
  • Which built-in marketplaces are enabled or disabled
  • Any custom websites you add

This is kept using Chrome's storage API. If you are signed in to Chrome with sync enabled, these settings may sync across your own devices through your Google account. They are never sent to the developer or any third party.

Website access

To know when to show a reminder, the extension checks the address (hostname) of the page you are visiting against your list of shopping sites. It does not read, record, modify, or transmit the content of any web page, and it does nothing on pages that are not on your list.

No tracking

There are no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, no accounts, and no external servers. The extension makes no network requests of its own.

External links

Each du'a links to its source on sunnah.com. Opening one of these links is no different from clicking any other link and is governed by that website's own privacy policy.

Children's privacy

Because no personal data is collected from anyone, the extension is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
